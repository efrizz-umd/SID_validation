Restart files are located at our zenodo repository:
LINK HERE


Download and unzip the data archive to gain access to all of the restart files used in the paper.
You can than point the 'in.shock*' scripts in the shock folder to the prepared channel you'd like to shock (or tap, or settle).
Alternatively, you can generate your own prepared beds using 'in.fill' in the bed preparation folder, then tapping ('in.tap') the bed if desired,
or going straight to bed settling ('in.settle') in the tapping folder, before finally shocking the prepared bed.
